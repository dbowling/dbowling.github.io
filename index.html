Our love story

How Robin and Dan came to this day...

Our first, first date
2012

Our first date was playing cribbage at the iconic Bernice's Bakery.

Our second, first date
2015

We met again in three years later—and this time around we fell in love!

How he proposed
August 2017

Circling back to their first date, Dan proposed at Bernice's Bakery, surprising Robin with a ring, a hand-crafted cribbage board to start their lives together, and some cupcakes to celebrate!

Since then we are together
To present

We make Missoula our home, where Dan is a Senior Application Solutions Engineer at the University of Montana-Missoula, and Robin is the Public Policy & Legal Director at the Montana Coalition Against Domestic & Sexual Violence.

We are so very excited to show our family and friends all of the special moments of Montana summers.
